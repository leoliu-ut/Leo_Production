Immersive Filmmaking


I'm obssessed with technology and computer visuals.

VR project
The work is a combination of the eastern harmony (Taoism) and the western aesthetics (Abstractionism). It reflects the duality within myself, depressed yet cheerful, upset yet calm, uncertain yet organized. A black and white yin-yang symbol represents my visualization of a world that is ever-changing. Official selection of the ARTSxSDGS Festival. Created with Adobe After Effects and MAYA.
